Concept

I am making an app that will work as a helper program to make my game easier to understand for new players and display the collection of beasts that are part of the game.

Platform

Android and iOS

Aesthetics

The application will have a simplistic design and a dark color theme. The music will be calm fantasy background music. AI will be used to generate art images for each beast. Below is an example of 3 dragon beasts.

Features

  • Tutorial: A page containing a short tutorial on how to play the game.
  • Beast Collection: A database of beasts in the game, with efficient navigation and filtering options by Element and Species.
  • Beast Display: A feature that displays individual beast cards and display their information including the name, species, element, special skill, Health points, Attack Power as well as an illustration of the beast.
